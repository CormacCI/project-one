Rugby Corca Dhuibhne

We are a local sporting, rugby club, that serves all the communities of west kerry. We are a sporting out let for all ages from the age of 6 upwards, our season, cover mainly the winter months from September to early May. We obviously develop Rugby in the area, but offer a team sport for all sizes and abilities, we offer a community within a community where all school kids, and adults from the parishes of west kerry play under the one umbrella

Facts

Rugby Corca Dhuibhne is the most westerly rugby club in Europe. Despite being relatively small in comparison to neighbouring clubs, the club has a strong membership. The club's senior team participates at the Junior 3 level in Munster. A few members from the club have gone on to represent Munster at various age groups.
